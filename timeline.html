Foundation

1960

François Le Lionnais and Raymond Queneau establish OuLiPo at Séminaire de Littérature Expérimentale, marking the beginning of constrained writing as a formal practice.

Raymond Queneau publishes ten sonnets with interchangeable lines, capable of producing 100 trillion unique poems.
